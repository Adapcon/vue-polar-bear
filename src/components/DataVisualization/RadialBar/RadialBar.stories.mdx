# **PbRadialBar**

### Description

The `PbRadialBar` component enables the representation of a radial progress bar.

### Purpose and Use Case

The `PbRadialBar` component showcases a visually appealing colored progress bar in a radial format.
The bar can be equipped with labels to facilitate user comprehension of their current step within a process.
This component is intended to provide users with a visual indication of their progress during specific actions.
