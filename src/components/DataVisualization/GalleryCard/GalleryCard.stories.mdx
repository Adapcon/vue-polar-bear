# **PbGalleryCard**
### The `PbGalleryCard` componente provide a flexible and extensible content container.

### Purpose and Use Case

A `PbGalleryCard` is an expansive content container.
Having the possibility to change your background.

Its `title` and `subtitle` are attached to the expanding footer,
if they exceed the natural size of the card, an ellipse will be added (...)
and when expanded, both will appear in full.
