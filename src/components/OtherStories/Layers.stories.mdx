# **PbLayers**
#

To organize and order your elements in *Z axis* you should always use these special css classes:


### **Layer Underlay**
#### `<... class="layer-underlay">`
The **layer underlay** applies a `z-index: -1` to the element.

---

### **Layer Base**
#### `<... class="layer-base">`
The **layer base** applies a `z-index: 10` to the element.

---

### **Layer Menu**
#### `<... class="layer-menu">`
The **layer menu** applies a `z-index: 20` to the element.

---

### **Layer Overlay**
#### `<... class="layer-overlay">`
The **layer overlay** applies a `z-index: 30` to the element.

---

### **Layer Modal**
#### `<... class="layer-modal">`
The **layer modal** applies a `z-index: 40` to the element.

---

### **Layer Always on Top**
#### `<... class="layer-always-on-top">`
The **layer always on top** applies a `z-index: 50` to the element.

---
